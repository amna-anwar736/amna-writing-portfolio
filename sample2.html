Nobody told me building a website was the easy part. The hard part? Getting people to actually find it. I spent weeks perfecting a client's WordPress site — clean design, fast loading, mobile-friendly. Then it sat at page 6 on Google for 3 months. Here's what changed everything: → Targeted 3 long-tail keywords instead of 1 broad one → Rewrote every meta description to actually be interesting → Built 10 internal links across existing pages → Fixed 4 technical errors Google Search Console was flagging Within 6 weeks — page 1 for 2 of the 3 keywords. A business owner asked me last week: "Do I really need a Moodle LMS? Can't I just post videos on YouTube?" Here's the honest answer: YouTube is free. Moodle costs time and setup. But YouTube gives you zero control over: ✗ Who watches your content ✗ How students progress ✗ Whether they actually learn ✗ Your revenue (ads can kill your income overnight) Moodle gives you: ✓ A private, branded learning space ✓ Student enrollment & progress tracking ✓ Quizzes, assignments, certificates ✓ Complete ownership of your content If you're serious about eLearning — you need the right platform. YouTube is for discovery. Moodle is for transformation. #MoodleLMS #eLearning #OnlineCourses #EdTech
